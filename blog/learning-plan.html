Bootcamp Learning Plan (UPDATED!)



What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?



In terms of a long-term goal, I’d say that my focus is on building businesses with positive environmental and social impacts using technology. For me learning to code is a key element of that and I see dev academy as part of a wider learning journey that I hope will lead into multiple other code languages, while allowing me to build websites, apps and tools that faciliate this growth.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?



Probably one of my biggest weaknesses is around getting distracted easily and struggling to plan ahead effectively. Learning how to timebox properly is a key element of this and something that I intend to include as a key focus area throughout my time at dev academy. Additionally, I also have some issues with emotional instability and feeling down quite regularly. I intend to manage this carefully and make sure I do several positive, uplifting things each day to maintain an even keel.
After doing foundations I’d say my biggest strength is going to be pushing through and getting things done, even when it feels extremely challenging if not impossible. I feel like I’ve done well given the challenges I’ve faced during this time and still managed to complete at least the bare minimum - which is an achievement given that a lot of my time was taken up by external factors.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?



I would say definitely time management and not getting distracted by other things that feel urgent. I guess I really need to focus on building up my skills around this and ability to focus as I am committed to succeeding at the course and it sits at a high priority for me.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?



I think for me improving my time-management and planning skills would be a really powerful area to develop and help to overcome some of the current biggest weaknesses. Additionally I would say that it’s important for me to improve my emotional challenge, capacity to cope with stress and generally work alongside others in a high-pressure context.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?



I expect clear instructions about the tasks that need to be completed, timelines, as well as support and understanding when required.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp



That I will absolutely try my best to succeed - give my 110%, ask for help when I need to and always keep trying even when it seems difficult.

I am entirely committed to succeeding in this program and will manage my workload by dedicating the next few months of my life to achieving the learning competencies.
