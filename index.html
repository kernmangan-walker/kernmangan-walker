About me

Last updated: 23 November 2021

Hello there! I'm Kern Mangan-Walker,

I'm currently based out of Whakatane. I moved here for the sunshine, the surf, epic walks and the quietness while I study over the summer months. It's been a wild time this past year but I'm so pleased to be part of this epic learning community.

Currently I co-lead a couple of businesses (post-consumer plastic recycling & manufacturing + early learning hardware tech) where coding (Javascript, React) is fairly crucial but have previously worked with outsourcing agencies where things haven't gone as smoothly as our team would have liked. I'm keen to build up my coding knowledge experience so I can do more of the development myself as well as being able to work more closely and effectively with any external dev agencies that we may work with. Generally, I'm also in love with the Kaupapa of Dev Academy and feel there is so much within the course that will help me to learn and grow as a person as well as becoming a developer.

Some of my favourite things include:

Great conversations, good friendships, surfing, bike rides, drawing, listening to music, looking at the stars, brainstorming ideas, reading/writing, hiking and spending time with animals.
